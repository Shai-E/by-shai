Privacy Policy for Momma Games

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

Momma Games ("the App") is a collection of classic games provided free of charge. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

The App is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect personal data from users.

Information We Collect

Momma Games does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Location information
  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

Local Data Storage

The App may store limited information locally on your device to support normal gameplay.

This data may include:

  • Game progress
  • Game state
  • Scores or settings

This information:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is not transmitted to the developer or any third party
  • Is used solely to allow the games to resume where you left off

If you uninstall the App, this data may be deleted depending on your device settings.

Donations

Momma Games may include a voluntary donation option to support development.

If you choose to make a donation, payment processing is handled entirely by the platform provider (such as the Apple App Store or Google Play). The developer does not receive or store your payment information.

Please refer to the privacy policies of the relevant platform provider for information about how payment data is handled.

Advertising

Momma Games does not display advertisements and does not use advertising networks.

Third-Party Services

Momma Games does not integrate third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs.

However, the app is distributed through third-party platforms (such as app stores), which may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Momma Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

Because the App does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly process information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
